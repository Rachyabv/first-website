ABOUT ABVEE FASHION DESIGN ACADEMY

Best Fashion School in Lagos.

ABVee Fashion Academy is a fashion design school based in Lekki Ajah Lagos,Nigeria.
We offer fashion design classes to fit every need whether you are a Secondary School
Student preparing to enter into a University, a Graduate, a Fashion Professional seeking
additional training, anxious to start/launch your fashion business/line/collection, or a fashion
enthusiast looking to learn more about fashion just for the fun of it.
We have created an environment where students can learn about globally respected high fashion
and master the skills necessary to succeed in fashion. Since our opening, we have trained over 5000 students
and strive to bring real-world technical skills from the industry into the classroom allowing students to jump-start
their career in a fashion faster than ever by promoting creativity and imagination and teaching proved industry techniques.
